    Freight Handler

    •      Load freight into trailers ensuring full utilization of space provided

    Fire Extinguisher Technician

    •      Service of portable fire extinguishers, fire
    hose reels
    •      Identify any defects, faults or rectification requirements
    •      Complete all relevant paperwork for service and repair work
    •      Assist with the cleaning and daily upkeep
    •      Equipment checking and maintenance
    •      Loading / unloading using forklifts
    •      Quality control and inspection of goods

  • 2011 - Current

    Automotive Electrician

    •      Advanced and technical repair of electrical and electronic components of public transport bus's.

    •      Leading hand role while current leading hand is on leave, in this role I oversaw work flow, organized staff and work tasks, ordering and maintenance of stock levels and communicated with fellow employees to maintain an orderly and effectively safe working environment.
    •      Logging of work orders and times.
    •      Prioritization of tasks.
    •      Use of workflow based computer programs, including, ms office, ms outlook, trim, and ellipse.
